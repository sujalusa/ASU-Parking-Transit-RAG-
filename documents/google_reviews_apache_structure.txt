★★★★★ — "The best-kept secret on campus. Stays open until almost 11am when every other structure is full by 9. Yes it's on the south edge by the light rail, but it's a 10 minute walk to most central buildings and I NEVER circle for a spot. Worth the walk."

★★★★☆ — "If you have a 10:30 class this is your garage. People avoid it because they think it's 'far' but it's right on the light rail line so you can also just hop one stop. Reliable availability is worth way more than 5 minutes of walking."

★★★☆☆ — "Fine structure, fills around 10:30-11. The walk to the engineering buildings is a bit much in the heat. But compared to fighting for a Tyler St spot it's a no-brainer in the morning."

★★★★☆ — "Closest structure to the light rail station which makes it perfect for a park-and-ride hybrid. Park here, and if my class is on the north end I'll take the rail one stop instead of walking. Underrated combo."

★★☆☆☆ — "It's genuinely far if your classes are on the north side of campus by Coor and Durham. Fine for the south/athletics side. Know your building locations before you assume this works for you."
